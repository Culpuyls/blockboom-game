Privacy Policy for BlockBoom

Effective Date: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BlockBoom ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile game (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

BlockBoom is designed to be played without requiring user registration or accounts. We do not store personal details on external databases. However, the game interacts with third-party services that may collect data:

  • Data for Advertising (via Google AdMob): AdMob collects data such as your device's Advertising ID and ad interactions to serve relevant advertising.
  • Diagnostics Data: We may collect anonymous crash and error information to improve app stability and game performance.
  • Local Storage: Your high scores, game state, and puzzle progress are saved locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide App Functionality: To localise preferences and keep track of your scores on your device.
  • For Advertising: To display ads that allow the game to remain free to play.
  • For Improvements: To analyze performance metrics and fix technical bugs.

3. Data Encryption and Security

All information transferred to our advertising partners (Google AdMob) is encrypted in transit using HTTPS, adhering to industry security standards.

4. Data Deletion and Retention

Since we do not collect or store personal user accounts or data on external servers, there is no personal data retention on our side. Uninstalling the mobile application from your device will permanently clear all your locally stored game data and scores.
